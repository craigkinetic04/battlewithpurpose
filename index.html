Their Story
A Family That Changed Everything
On Saturday, April 11, 2026, a tragic accident in Moulton, Alabama took the lives of 33-year-old Ashley Hagood and two of her children — 13-year-old Bryant and 7-year-old Bryleigh. They were a beautiful family, full of life, love, and laughter.

Two children survived. Brynleigh is still fighting — on life support with brain swelling, surrounded by everyone who loves her. Bentley woke up to a world turned upside down. He is awake, hurting, and scared — asking questions no child should ever have to ask.

Bentley and Brynleigh still need our support — for medical bills, funeral costs, and the long road ahead. The TikTok LIVE creator community has always shown up for what matters. This tournament is our answer. Battle with Purpose.

Tournament Rules
Register by Friday Apr 17
Registration closes Friday April 17 at 11:59 PM EST. No late entries. Matching begins Saturday April 18 at midnight EST.
5-Minute Battles
Each battle uses TikTok LIVE's native 5-minute battle timer. Both creators must be live simultaneously. Only gifts during the timer count.
Highest Diamonds Wins
The creator with the highest total diamond value from gifts wins. Kinetic enters the final score from TikTok's official scoreboard.
Single Elimination
Lose and you're out. Win and you advance. Day 1 Qualifiers, Day 2 Semifinals, Day 3 Championship.
Follow Both Accounts
You MUST follow @the_mr.rogers and @_wheeler_hill on TikTok before Sunday. Once you receive your matchup, you must also follow your matched creator so you can co-host the battle. TikTok requires mutual follows to start a battle.
Turn Off 18+ Filter
Go to TikTok Settings → Content Preferences and turn OFF the 18+ content filter. If this is on, TikTok will block you from co-hosting a battle. Also make sure you and your matched creator follow each other — both are required to start a battle.
75% Pledge Required
All creators pledge to donate a minimum 75% of their diamond earnings to the Hagood family via Venmo @Trevorshairbnb. No pledge = no entry.
Champion Recognition
The tournament champion is featured across all Kinetic Live Agency platforms and permanently honored in the Battle with Purpose Hall of Fame.
